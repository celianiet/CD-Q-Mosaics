CD&Q Mosaicos specializes in Glass and Ceramic Mosaic, proposing this antique tradition in a unique manner where an outstanding quality in product blends together with an efficient and fast service to the customers.

CD&Q team of professionals, with more than 20 years in the mosaic industry, has vast experience; our specialty is the creation of exclusive customized products where our clients can set their individual and unique touch.
